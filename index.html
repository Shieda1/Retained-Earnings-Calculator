What is the ending retained earnings calculater?
The retained earnings calculator is a tool that calculates how much money a company has left to reinvest into its business.
How to calculate ending retained earnings?
To calculate ending retained earnings you should use the following formula:
ending retained earnings = starting retained earnings + net income - dividends
Why use ending retained earnings calculater?
The retained earnings calculater is used in order to analyse the financial health of the company.
